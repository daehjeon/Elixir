Description

On your way grocery shopping this weekend, try to make eye contact and smile at 3 people on the way there and the way back. Purpose Making eye contact is one way to enter a conversation. Also this task allows me to interact with strangers without having to speak to them. Coping skills Use 5-4-3-2-1 strategy (see, feel hear) Things to avoid Try not to avoid people by isolating yourself (e.g. sitting in an empty section on the bus)
